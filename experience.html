David Pretlow aka Dave DeMonki.

I currently live in Atlanta.

Experience

Since 1993 I have been playing music for a living. I played violin throughout my childhood ane trained with Classical Sympohony musicians in Birmingham, AL, Boston, MA, and Cleveland, OH as a violinist. In high school I began teaching myself bass guitar and acoustic guitar. After graduating college in 1992 I got an oppportunity to help form a band and move to Destin, Florida to perform in a restaurant 5 nights a week.

After several years of hard work on my guitar and singing skills, I began performing as a solo guitar player in a singer-songwriter format. In 2005 I co-founded a band called "Beach Mice" with a friend and business partner. In "Beach Mice" I performed on guitar and electric violin. We had several good years where we played on the beach several days a week in the Spring and Summer seasons and worked in the Jimmy Buffett owned clubs/restaurants called "Margaritaville."

Hobbies

Becoming a professional musician blurred the lines between music as a hobby and music as a job. Although I still love playing and music in general, performing for a living has changed my relationship with music significantly. For my hobbies I enjoy cycling, cooking, and traveling.

Volunteer

For the past 3 years I have enjoyed voluteering weekly to teach a guitar class at a local Senior Center
